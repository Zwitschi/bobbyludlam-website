# Portfolio

## Open Mic Odyssey

Open Mic Odyssey is an upcoming feature film centered on three friends entering the unpredictable world of open mic comedy. Public descriptions connect Bobby Ludlam with Georg Sinn and Corey Pellizzi, positioning the project around friendship, performance, and the disorder that comes with chasing comedy seriously.

- Status: Post-production
- Expected release window: 2026
- Suggested external link: https://openmicodyssey.com/

## Bobby Ludlam's Inter-dimensional Safe Space

Bobby Ludlam's Inter-dimensional Safe Space is a creative initiative imagining a protected sanctuary for peaceful coexistence between humans, nature, extraterrestrials, Bigfoot, cryptids, and other allegedly mythical beings.

The concept combines comedy, conservation language, education, and community imagination. It is best presented as an active concept and fundraiser, not as a completed physical site.

- Status: Fundraising / concept development
- Suggested external link: https://www.gofundme.com/f/support-bobby-ludlams-interdimensional-safe-space
