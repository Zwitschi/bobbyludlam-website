# Biography

## Short Bio

Bobby Ludlam is an Austin-based stand-up comedian, writer, artist, and creator whose work blends live comedy, documentary storytelling, visual imagination, and offbeat big-idea projects.

## Full Bio

Bobby Ludlam is a stand-up comedian, writer, artist, and creator currently based in Austin, Texas. His creative path has moved through online comedy, open mic performance, collaborative filmmaking, and community-driven projects that balance absurdity with sincerity.

Before Austin, Bobby was associated with Ridgecrest, California and Bolingbrook, Illinois. Across those chapters he built a public trail through comedy videos, writing, open mic appearances, and creative experiments shaped by curiosity, resilience, and an outsider point of view.

Bobby is connected to Open Mic Odyssey, an upcoming feature film about three friends navigating the world of open mic comedy. The project places him inside a larger story about performance, friendship, and the chaos of trying to build a creative life.

He is also the creator behind Bobby Ludlam's Inter-dimensional Safe Space, a project built around peaceful coexistence between humans, nature, extraterrestrials, cryptids, and other supposedly impossible visitors. The concept is humorous on the surface, but the underlying themes are consistent: curiosity, nonviolence, conservation, education, and openness to the unknown.
